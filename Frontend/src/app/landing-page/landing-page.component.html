Analyse and predict the sentiment of tweets in Real time with AI and BIGDATA Technology, all with a dashboard to see exactly what goes around.

Overview of the Project

Welcome to our Twitter Sentiment Analysis project! Our goal is to harness the power of AI and Big Data technology to analyze and predict the sentiment of tweets in real-time. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and Apache Kafka Streams, we aim to provide users with valuable insights into the ever-changing landscape of social media sentiment.

Project Key Goals

Real-time Sentiment Analysis

Our primary objective is to analyze tweets as they are posted on Twitter, enabling users to stay updated on the sentiment trends in real-time.

Predictive Modeling

We aim to predict tweet sentiment accurately using models like Logistic Regression and XGBoost, discerning between positive, negative, neutral, or irrelevant sentiments.

User-Friendly Dashboard

We're committed to providing an intuitive dashboard interface that allows users to visualize sentiment analysis results effortlessly.
